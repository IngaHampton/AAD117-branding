A little more about Me!

Well hello there! I am Inga Hampton and welcome to my page. I currently am a first year Interaction Design Student at the University of Ulster, located in Belfast.

I have a great interest in most aspects of creative Arts; graphical art, music, traditional art and photography. I enjoy creating wallpapers and typography. I am greatly inspired by the works of Josef Muller-Brockmann and Paul Rand. I am a lover for the flat form design as i think it can portray an image within a few simple shapes. As I am woman of little words I like to use the flat form illustrations to help express and emphasise what I am trying to put across to my audience, As I said a picture can speak a throusand words.

In my spare time I enjoy playing games. I also like to draw and listen to a wide range of music. When I can, I love to go to concerts, watch comedy, travel... and the list goes on! For now keep a wee eye out, as I shall be sharing my thoughts and designs with you!

Thank for reading!
